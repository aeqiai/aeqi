# pack:content-campaign

Use this package when a company needs repeatable content, launch, or
marketing work. The default topology is supervisor plus producer-reviewer:
one lead owns the campaign, specialists produce lanes, and review gates keep
message quality consistent.

## Default roles

- Campaign Lead: owns audience, offer, message, channel plan, and cadence.
- Researcher: collects audience language, objections, competitors, and proof.
- Writer: drafts long-form, short-form, scripts, ads, or email.
- Visual Planner: produces thumbnail, image, layout, or creative briefs.
- Distribution Operator: maps channels, timing, owners, and measurement.
- QA Reviewer: checks claims, tone, compliance, and channel fit.

## Seed ideas

- Audience brief: who it is for and what they already believe.
- Message house: claim, proof, objections, and forbidden promises.
- Channel policy: where content belongs and how it should be adapted.
- Experiment log: variants, dates, results, and decisions.

## First quests

- Write the campaign brief.
- Draft the first asset set.
- Review against message and channel policy.
- Publish or schedule the approved assets.
- Capture results and update the experiment log.

## Done signal

The campaign has a visible asset set, a channel plan, a review record, and a
learning loop that can improve the next run.
