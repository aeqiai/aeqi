## Design Context

### Users
Developers, founders, and technical operators managing autonomous AI agent systems. They're in flow state — issuing commands, reading agent output, iterating fast. The chat input is their primary control surface. Every millisecond of friction costs focus.

### Brand Personality
**Precise. Authoritative. Beautiful.**

Japanese-German fusion — the restraint of wabi-sabi meets Bauhaus functionalism. Nothing decorative, nothing wasted. Every element earns its place through utility. Beauty emerges from perfect proportion and purposeful reduction.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Monochromatic light mode** — white canvas, black ink, opacity for hierarchy
- **Inter for UI, JetBrains Mono for code** — two typefaces, no more
- **4px spacing grid** — mathematical rhythm throughout
- **No color accents** — status colors (green/red/amber/blue) only when semantically required
- **No shadows for decoration** — depth only where it communicates layering
- **Transitions exist for continuity**, not delight — 150ms, never bouncy

### Anti-references
- Gradient backgrounds, glassmorphism, neon accents
- Rounded bubbly interfaces (Notion, Linear's softer elements)
- Anything that feels like "AI wrapper startup aesthetic"
- Excessive whitespace that wastes screen real estate
- Skeleton loaders, confetti, emoji reactions

### Design Principles

1. **Density over decoration** — Pack information tightly. Professionals scan, they don't browse. Every pixel communicates.
2. **Black ink on white paper** — The palette is monochromatic by conviction, not limitation. Color is reserved for meaning.
3. **The input is the interface** — The composer is the most important element. It should feel like a precision instrument, not a text box.
4. **Quiet confidence** — No element begs for attention. Authority comes from restraint and perfect alignment.
5. **Japanese negative space, German grid** — Breathing room is intentional and mathematically placed, never arbitrary.
